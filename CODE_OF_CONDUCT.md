# Code of Conduct

This project is intended to be a respectful, practical space for improving
child-safe AI application patterns.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific in technical discussions.
- Assume contributors may have different backgrounds in Android, backend,
  AI safety, or child product design.
- Keep feedback focused on code, documentation, safety, and user impact.
- Treat child privacy and family safety concerns as high priority.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing private child or family data.
- Encouraging unsafe product behavior for children.
- Publishing secrets, credentials, or private operational details.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate
these expectations. Repeated or serious violations may result in being blocked
from participating in the project.
